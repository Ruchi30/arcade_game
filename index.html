Arcade Game Guideline

How to Play

Use UP, DOWN, LEFT and RIGHT keys for moving the player. Avoid the bug. Player will get rating according to time spent and score

Instructions

  • Touch the water
  • collect the gems
  • collect the life

Point & Life details

  • Green Gem: 10 point (appear when score is more than 10)
  • Orange Gem: 15 point (appear when score is more than 20)
  • Star: 20 point (appear when score is more than 30)
  • Heart: 1 Life (appear when score is more than 15)
